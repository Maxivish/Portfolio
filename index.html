Education

I began my academic journey at K.B.L. English High School in Mumbai, where I achieved an SSC score of 83.80%. It was during this time that I developed a strong interest in mathematics and logic, which laid the foundation for my problem-solving skills.
Following this, I attended R.K. Talreja College for my HSC, securing a score of 79.85%. My fascination with computer science and technology grew during this phase, solidifying my decision to pursue a career in Information Technology.
To further my expertise, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Xavier Institute of Engineering, graduating with a CGPA of 7.51. This program provided me with a solid understanding of programming, web development, and modern technological tools, complemented by hands-on experience through various projects. Each step of my education has been instrumental in shaping my technical foundation and preparing me for a career in the dynamic field of technology.
